Medical Scientist | Msc Bioinformatics Candidate
------------------------------------------------

I am a Medical Scientist and MSc Bioinformatics student. My interests lie at the intersection of biological sciences, computational analysis, and data-driven healthcare solutions.

My academic and research experience spans molecular diagnostics and infectious disease surveillance. I have worked on projects involving poliovirus surveillance, hepatitis seroprevalence studies, diagnostic assay validation, molecular characterization of viral pathogens, and public health data analysis.

As a bioinformatics student, I am developing expertise in computational biology, genomics, statistical genetics, data science, and biological data analysis. I enjoy applying computational approaches to understand complex biological systems and generate insights from large-scale datasets.

This Github serves as a document my learning journey, share research-related projects, explore computational methods, and showcase work at the intersection of biology and technology.

Research Areas:

🧬 Immunology

🦠 Virology

🧪 Molecular Diagnostics

📊 Data Science

💻 Bioinformatics

🌍 Public Health Research

I am passionate about using computational tools and biological data to address real-world health challenges and contribute to the advancement of precision medicine and public health.

“Transforming biological data into meaningful scientific discoveries.”
